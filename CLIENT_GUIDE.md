# Niche Design Studio Website — Client Guide

Welcome! This guide explains how to edit your website content using **Sanity Studio**—a simple, visual editor built specifically for your site.

---

## Getting Started

### Accessing Sanity Studio

1. **Go to your studio login page:**
   ```
   https://your-studio-domain.sanity.studio
   ```
   *(You'll receive the exact URL and login credentials separately)*

2. **Sign in** with your email and password
3. You'll see the **Sanity Studio dashboard** with different sections to edit

---

## Main Content Areas

### 1. **Navigation Menu** (Header Links)

This is the menu that appears at the top of every page.

**To edit:**
1. Click **"Settings"** in the left sidebar
2. Scroll to **"Navigation Links"**
3. Click the pencil icon next to any link to edit
4. Change the label (e.g., "ABOUT", "PORTFOLIO") or URL
5. To add a new link, click **"Add Navigation Link"**
6. To remove a link, click the **X** button
7. Click **"Publish"** at the top to save changes

**Changes appear on your site within seconds.**

---

### 2. **Home Page (Hero Section)**

The large image and text at the top of your homepage.

**To edit:**
1. Click **"Home"** in the left sidebar
2. You'll see fields for:
   - **Hero Image** — Click to upload or change the image
   - **Headline** — Edit the main text
   - **Subheading** — Edit the supporting text
3. Make your changes
4. Click **"Publish"** to save

**Tips:**
- Images should be at least 1200px wide for best quality
- Keep headlines short and impactful

---

### 3. **About Page**

Your studio story and background.

**To edit:**
1. Click **"Pages"** → **"About"** in the sidebar
2. Edit the sections:
   - **Title** — Page heading
   - **Body Text** — Main content (use formatting tools like bold, italics)
   - **Images** — Click to add or swap images
3. Click **"Publish"** when done

---

### 4. **Portfolio**

Your project showcase.

**To edit individual projects:**
1. Click **"Portfolio"** in the sidebar
2. Click the project you want to edit
3. Edit:
   - **Title** — Project name
   - **Description** — What you did
   - **Category** — Type of work (Brand, Web, etc.)
   - **Images** — Add or replace project images
   - **URL** — Link to live project (optional)
4. Click **"Publish"**

**To add a new project:**
1. Click **"Portfolio"** → **"Create New"** button
2. Fill in all fields
3. Click **"Publish"**

**To remove a project:**
1. Open the project
2. Click the **three-dot menu** (⋮) at the top
3. Select **"Delete"**

---

### 5. **Contact Page**

Your contact form and information.

**To edit:**
1. Click **"Pages"** → **"Contact"** in the sidebar
2. Edit:
   - **Title** — Page heading
   - **Email Address** — Where inquiries go
   - **Phone** (optional)
   - **Social Links** — Your social profiles
3. Click **"Publish"**

---

### 6. **Settings**

General site configuration.

**What you can edit here:**
- **Navigation Links** — Your menu items (see above)
- **Site Title** — Name of your studio
- **Footer Text** — Copyright info or closing message
- **Social Media Links** — Instagram, LinkedIn, etc.

---

## How to Make Changes

All edits follow the same pattern:

1. **Find the section** you want to edit in the left sidebar
2. **Click to open it**
3. **Make your changes** in the editing panel
4. **Click "Publish"** to save and make it live

**That's it!** Your changes appear on the website almost instantly.

## Tips & Best Practices

### Text Editing
- Use the **formatting toolbar** for bold, italics, links
- Keep content concise—white space is design
- Proofread before publishing

### Images
- **Recommended sizes:**
  - Hero images: 1200px wide or larger
  - Portfolio images: 800px–1200px wide
  - Thumbnails: at least 600px wide
- **File formats:** JPG or PNG (PNG for illustrations, JPG for photos)
- **Keep file size small:** Compress before uploading for faster loading

### Links
- When adding a portfolio link, use the full URL: `https://example.com`
- Internal page links work automatically (just type `/about` or `/contact`)

## Common Questions

### "I published changes but don't see them on the website"
- **Wait 5–10 seconds** — The site updates automatically but may take a moment
- **Refresh your browser** — Try clearing your cache (Cmd+Shift+R on Mac, Ctrl+Shift+R on Windows)
- **Check your URL** — Make sure you're looking at the right page

### "I accidentally deleted something—can I undo?"
- **Yes!** Click the **undo button** (⟲) at the top of Sanity Studio
- Or click the **History** tab to see past versions and restore them

### "Can I save drafts without publishing?"
- **Yes.** Don't click "Publish"—your changes stay as a **Draft**
- When you're ready, click **"Publish"** to make it live
- To resume editing a draft, open the content and make more changes

### "How do I add a new page?"
- Contact your designer. New pages require code changes, not just content editing

### "Can I see how changes look before publishing?"
- **Yes!** Click the **Preview** button (eye icon) to see your changes live
- Close the preview and come back to edit before publishing

1. **Check this guide** — Most questions are answered above
2. **Contact your designer** — We're happy to help!
3. **Don't worry about breaking anything** — You can always undo or restore previous versions

## What You Can't Edit (And Why)

Some parts of the site are locked:

- **Page layouts** — These require code changes (contact your designer)
- **Site colors/fonts** — These are part of your brand system
- **Form functionality** — Contact forms work automatically

These keep your site stable and looking consistent. If you want to change these things later, just ask!

---

## Need to Upload Files?

If you need to add a PDF, document, or other file:

1. Click the **asset/file icon** in the rich text editor
2. Upload your file
3. Copy the generated link
4. Paste it in your content

Or ask your designer—they can set this up for you.
